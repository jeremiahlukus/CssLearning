"Two kingdoms sit locked in a old war. Galinesk, driven by technology and resources has banned magic from their kingdom. Auraleis breathes magic from every corner of the realm. Meanwhile, in secrecy, deserters of the Auraleis special task force, are testing an ancient plague. They are led by the ex-queen of Auraleis, Odessa. Both countries have to overcome their differences and must band together in order to prevent the plague from overtaking and destroying the world they live in."
